Key New AI Visualization Tools & Their Usages
Julius AI: A chat-based analyst that creates data visualizations (charts, graphs) by answering questions in plain English, ideal for spreadsheets.
Napkin.ai: Transforms text-heavy reports into polished, branded visual graphics in seconds, ideal for business reports.
Tableau Pulse: Uses generative AI to deliver personalized insights and automated, context-aware visualizations.
ThoughtSpot: Employs AI-driven search analytics for natural language exploration of large datasets.
Microsoft Power BI Copilot: Integrates AI for automated data analysis and report generation.
Observable: A notebook-style, collaborative platform that suggests visualizations based on data patterns.
NVIDIA Canvas: Converts rough sketches into realistic, AI-generated images, focusing on visual concept exploration
